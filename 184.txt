
Eriocaulon pygmaeum
Nakshatrachutu is an annual herb of the Erythrococcus family. The plant is found in the tropics of Africa, the Himalayas, Malaysia and the Indian subcontinent. Red stone can be found in wetlands and fields during the rainy season. The rounded leaves of this stemless plant grow directly from the soil. The leaves are pointed. Leaves compound, star-shaped, rounded, glabrous. Seeds yellow, elongated.
