Fingerprint is a 2005 Malayalam film directed by Satish Paul and starring Jayaram, Indrajith, Nedumudi Venu and Gopika in the lead roles. Produced by Sabu Cherian under the banner of Anand Bhairavi, the film was distributed by Central Pictures. The story of this film is by Satish Paul. Screenplay and dialogues by Siddique.
The background music for the film is composed by Praveen Mani.
