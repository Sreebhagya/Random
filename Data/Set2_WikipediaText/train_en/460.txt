
Hardcore farming is a method of cultivation practiced on uncultivated waste lands. Due to its peculiarities, it requires a lot of investment, labor and overuse of pesticides. The attraction of hard farming is the high yield from low land. This method is widely used in paddy cultivation in Kerala. This leads to loss of natural structure of the soil due to excessive pesticide-fertilizer application.
