Kadavoor G was a prominent Malayalam playwright and cultural activist. Chandran Pillai. Won the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award for Drama in 1986.
He is the son of Gopal Pillai and Umminiamma of Kadavur, Kollam. After graduation he became active in the library movement and drama. He worked for a long time as the administrator of the Kollam Public Library. He has written novels and short stories along with plays.
