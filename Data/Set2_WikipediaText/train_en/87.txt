Bhanupriya is an actress from South India. Born into a Telugu family, Bhanupriya has acted in both Telugu, Tamil and Malayalam. Bhanupriya starred mainly in the 1980s and 1990s. She also acted in Bollywood in the 1990s. She currently resides in the United States and teaches Bharatanatyam and Kuchipudi at a dance school there. She has acted in more than 111 films. Bhanupriya was known as Sridevi of South India.
Bhanupriya made her film debut in the Telugu film Sithara. She has acted in over 25 Telugu films, 30 Tamil films and 14 Hindi films. Bhanupriya is a very talented person in Indian classical dance. Bhanupriya has done dance based roles in all her famous films.
Bhanupriya is married to Adarsh ​​Kaushal, a well-known photographer. They have a daughter.
