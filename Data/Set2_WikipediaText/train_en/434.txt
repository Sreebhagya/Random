Dhaipai is a village in the Ludhiana district in the state of Punjab. Dhaipai is located at a distance of 10 km from the district headquarters of Ludhiana. Sarpanch is the sovereign of Dhai Pai village. The Sarpanch is the representative elected by the people.
According to the 2011 Indian Census, there are 518 households in Dhaipai. The total population is 2,637. This includes 1418 males and 1219 females. The literacy rate in Dhaipai is 72.51 percent. This is below the state average of 75.84. The number of children under the age of 6 in Dhaipai is 279. This is 10.58 per cent of the total population of Dhaipai.
According to the 2011 census, 912 people are engaged in various occupations. Of these, 739 were males and 173 were females. According to the 2011 census, 88.49 per cent people consider their job as their main source of income but 52.52 per cent see their current job as a temporary source of income for the next 6 months.
All the 1320 persons in Dhaipai belong to the Scheduled Castes. 0 persons belong to Scheduled Tribes.
