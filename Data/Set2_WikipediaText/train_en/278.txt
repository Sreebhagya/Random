This culture was found in Algeria, Cyprus, France, Greece, Israel, Italy, Jordan, Lebanon, Libya, Malta, Morocco, Portugal, Spain, Syria, Tunisia and Turkey. The major cities of Phoenician culture were all on the shores of the Mediterranean. The Phoenicians were famous for their voyages and trade. They sailed on the ships of Gali. The warships Byrem were invented by the Phoenicians. They also monopolized the sale of purple pigment Murex, a marine snail.
