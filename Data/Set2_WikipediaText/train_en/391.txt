Rasool Bhai is a Hindustani singer. He has won the Kendra Sangeetha Nataka Academy Award.
She was born in 1902 in Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh to a poor family.
He started studying music at the age of five under Ustad Shammu Khan. He then learned music from Aashiq Khan and Ustad Najju Khan. The music was first performed at Dhananjaya Court. In 1957, he was awarded the Kendra Sangeet Nataka Akademi Award.
Died December 15, 1974.
