IBall is a Mumbai - based electronics company. The company began operations in 2001 with the development of the mouse. As of 2011, the company is launching 21 different products. The company's flagship products include computer peripherals, smartphones and tablets. Sandeep Parashrampura is the director of the company.
The company also imports hardware components for personal computers, as well as mice, CPU cabinets and webcams. Home-use and computer-friendly speakers, headphones, MP3 players, microphones, SMPS and LCD TVs. The company also manufactures monitors, USB flash drives, tablets and barcode scanners.
