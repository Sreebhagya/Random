The preview premiered on November 30, 2017, but was released in theaters on May 4, 2018. The shooting was completed in just 18 days. When the film, which was scheduled for 25 days, was completed, it took only 18 days to shoot.
The film revolves around the death of Vavachan Mestri in a fishing village called Chellanam in Ernakulam district. When he got home, his wife complained that his mother-in-law was wandering around.
The title of the film is Iso Mariam Youssef. In some Christian communities, especially among Catholics, a prayer is said in the ear of the dying person. This prayer is "Iso Mariam Youssef be with my soul". The story revolves around the death of Vavachan Mestri, who lived in a village on the banks of the Chellanam in Kochi. Filming began in August 2017. Initially it was decided that the shooting would take 35 days but it was completed in 18 days. Apart from the lead actress, the film also stars newcomers from the Chellanam area. Produced by Rajesh George Kulangara, the film was later acquired by director Aashiq Abu.
