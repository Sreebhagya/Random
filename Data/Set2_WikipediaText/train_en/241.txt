Khurshid Alam Khan was a senior Congress leader and former Union Minister. During his tenure as the Governor of Karnataka, he held additional charge of Kerala. Member of Lok Sabha for 15 years and Member of Rajya Sabha from 1974 to 1984. He is the nephew of former President Zakir Hussain.
Born in Farrukhabad, Uttar Pradesh. He holds a Masters Degree in History from the University of Agra. He received his higher education from the United States. He was a minister in the Indira and Rajiv ministries. He has worked in education, textiles, tourism, transportation and foreign affairs. He was a Rajya Sabha member from Uttar Pradesh from 1974 to 1984 and a Lok Sabha member from Farrukhabad from 1984 to 1989. He was the Governor of Goa and Karnataka.
He was the former Chancellor of Jamia Millia Islamia University.
