

SH 46 is a state highway in Kerala that starts in Kilimanoor and ends in Alangode. It is 10.7 km long. Starting from State Highway 1, which passes close to the Kilimanoor junction, take the MC Road on the Nagaroor - Vanchiyoor route to reach the Alangode junction and join National Highway 66.
