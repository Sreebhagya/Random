
Krishna Crown Scientific name: Clerodendrum paniculatum. Known by many names such as Hanuman Crown, Peru, Krishnamudi, Six-month-old plant, Kavadippoovu and Pegota, this plant usually grows in shady areas. The plant grows to a height of 1.5 m and has reddish-orange flowers. The large leaves are unique in that its flowers are used to decorate Thrikkakarayapan and to prepare the flower bed for Onam.
