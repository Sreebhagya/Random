Vadakkancherry Grama Panchayat is a gram panchayat, located in the Alathur block of Alathur taluk, Palakkad district in the Indian state of Kerala. Vadakancherry is a Grama Panchayat comprising of Vadakancherry One and Vadakancherry Two villages. The present Vadakancherry Panchayat was formed in 1960 by the amalgamation of Vadakancherry, Ayakkad and Mangalam panchayats. 37.88 sq. Km. The area of ​​the panchayat is bounded by North Kannampra, Kavassery, Alathur Panchayats, East Vandazhi, East Kancherry, Alathur, Melarkode Panchayats, South East Kancherry, Kannampra Panchayats and West Kannampra Panchayat. Vadakkancherry is one of the largest trading centers in the district. In the past, migrants came from the southern districts - behind the present economy of North Kerala. It is also the largest producer of rubber and other spices in the district.
