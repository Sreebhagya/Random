Khisargarh is a village in the Shahibsada Ajit Singh Nagar district in the state of Punjab. Khisargarh is located at a distance of 10 km from the district headquarters of Shahibsada Ajit Singh Nagar. Sarpanch is the sovereign of Khisargarh village. The Sarpanch is the representative elected by the people.
According to the 2011 Indian Census, there are 310 households in Khisargarh. The total population is 1710. This includes 888 men and 822 women. The literacy rate in Khisargarh is 73.33%. This is below the state average of 75.84. The number of children under the age of 6 in Khisargarh is 217. This is 12.69 per cent of the total population of Khisargarh.
According to the 2011 census, 544 people are employed in various occupations. Of these, 508 were men and 36 were women. According to the 2011 census, 89.89 per cent people consider their job as their main source of income but 39.15 per cent see their current job as a temporary source of income for the next 6 months.
All the 543 people in Khisargarh belong to the Scheduled Castes. 0 persons belong to Scheduled Tribes.
