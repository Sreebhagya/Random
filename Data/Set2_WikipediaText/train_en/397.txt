Shinjuku is one of the 23 special wards in Tokyo, Japan. The Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building and Shinjuku Station, the world's busiest railway station, are located in this important financial city, the administrative headquarters of the Tokyo government. As of 2008, the 18.23 km2 city of Shinjuku had a population of 312,418.
Shinjuku Ward is located between Chioda Ward on the east, Bunkyo and Toshima Wards on the North, Nakano Ward on the West and Shibuya and Minato Wards on the South. The highest point in Shinjuku is the 1200 m high Hakoneyama in Toyama Park. .
Shinjuku has sister city agreements with many places.
