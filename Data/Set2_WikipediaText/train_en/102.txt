Nilapakshi is a 2000 Malayalam film directed by Anil Babu. The film stars Kunchacko Boban, Sneha and Sujitha in the lead roles. This is the first film of South Indian actress Sneha. Salil Chowdhury's sons Sanjay and Anthara have composed the music for the film.
Lyricist is Yusufali Kecheri and composers are Sanjay Salil Chowdhury and Anthara Salil Chowdhury. The songs are marketed by Satyam Audios.
