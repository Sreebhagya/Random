Shamma Jain, a senior Indian diplomat and Indian Ambassador to Greece since June 2017, was previously the Indian Ambassador to Panama, Costa Rica and Nicaragua. He served as India's Ambassador to Ivory Coast, Liberia, Sierra Leone and Guinea from 2008 to 2011. She has been the Deputy Chief of Mission in Rome, Italy, the Political Counselor in the US, the Permanent Representative of India to UNESCO in Paris, and has made numerous diplomatic legislations.
