Veer Singh (born 6 December 1972 in Amritsar - 10 June 1957) is a Punjabi poet and religious scholar .He is also known as Bhai Veer Singh.
Veer Singh was awarded the Sahitya Akademi Award in 1955 and the Padma Bhushan Award in 1956 for his contributions to literature.
In November 1899 he started the Punjabi weekly edition Khalsa Samachar. Shri Guru Granth Kosh, a revised edition of Ghani Hazare Singh's Dictionary, published in 1898, was republished in 1927. Sikhan the Bhagat Mala, Prachin Pant Prakash, Purathan Jan Sakhi and Sakhi Pothi are some of the other works.
