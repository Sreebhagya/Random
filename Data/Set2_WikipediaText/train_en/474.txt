Bakkalam is a small village on the eastern side of Morazha village in Kannur district, India.
There have been many struggles against feudalism in Bakkal. In 1936, farmers led by P Krishnapillai and Keralites marched from Bakkalam to Karakattidam to reduce rent and rent and end violence. Bakkalam was the center of many agitations such as the Mangattuparambu grassroots agitation and the Mangattuparambu food production collective agitation.
