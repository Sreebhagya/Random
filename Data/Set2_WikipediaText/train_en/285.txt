Jagesh Edakkad is a Keralite painter. In 2017, he received the Kerala Lalitha Kala Akademi Award for his work 'Glance from Past-7'.
A native of Edakkad in Kannur district, Jagesh won the Kerala Lalithakala Academy State Award in 2012 and the Kollam Siddhartha Foundation Honorary Mention in 2014. She was also awarded the Lalithakala Academy Student Scholarship in 2004 and 2010.
