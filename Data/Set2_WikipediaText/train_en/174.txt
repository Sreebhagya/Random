Chandraprava Saikiani was an Indian social reformer and writer from Assam. Chandraprabha Saikiani is the founder of the All Assam Local Women's Committee, an NGO working for the welfare of women in Assam. She was awarded the Padma Shri by the Government of India in 1972. Two decades later, in 2002, the Government of India issued the Saikiani Commemorative Stamp under the series of Social Revolutionaries.
Chandraprabha Saikiani was born on March 16, 1901 in Daisingiri, a small village in the Kamrup district of the northeastern Indian state of Assam.
