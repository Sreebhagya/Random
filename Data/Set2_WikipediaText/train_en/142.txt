Chittoor Government College is one of the oldest and most renowned educational institutions in Kerala. The college is located on the banks of the river Shokanashinipuzha in the city center of Chittoor in Palakkad district. Undergraduate and postgraduate courses are available here. There is also a Tamil research unit here. With the approval of Kozhikode University, Chittoor College continues its journey with 1400 students.
