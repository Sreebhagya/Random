Assembly elections were held on February 15, 2017. At one point, the verdict was for 69 seats. The verdict in Karnaprayag constituency has been postponed to March 9, 2017 due to the death of BSP candidate Kuldeep Kanwasi in a road accident. Neither party won a majority in the 2012 elections. The government was formed by the Indian National Congress with the support of the PDF party led by Vijay Bahuguna. Turnout was 65.64% in the February 15 election. Major parties contesting the 2017 elections: Samajwadi Party, Indian National Congress, Bahujan Samaj Party, Bharatiya Janata Party, Uttarakhand Kranti Dal, Independents.
