
Atrophaneura aristolochiae
The native rose is a very common butterfly in Kerala. It is equally common in rural and urban areas. Black on forewings, four or five white spots on hind wings. It bears a striking resemblance to the butterfly and the canary. This butterfly is also known as Chakra Rose. Foods: Liver, Ishwaramulla, Aaduthodappala
