The Architectural Work of Le Corbusier is a compilation of 17 architectural works by the French-Swiss architect Le Corbusier. These constructions show how modernity is used in construction. It also reflects the international standard of a sculptor. The UNESCO World Heritage List includes works by Courbasier from France, Switzerland, Japan, India, Germany, Belgium and Argentina.
