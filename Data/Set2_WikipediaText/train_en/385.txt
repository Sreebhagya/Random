Khushboo Sundar, better known as Khushboo Khan, is an Indian film actress, producer and television presenter.
Khushboo started her acting career as a child actor in the 1980s. His first film was Thodisi Bewafai. In 1981, he played a major role in the film Lavaris. Later she acted in more than a hundred films. He has done many roles in Tamil with leading actors Rajinikanth, Kamal Haasan, Sathyaraj, Prabhu, Suresh Gopi, Mohanlal, Mammootty, Jayaram and Dileep.
Apart from Tamil films, she has acted in a number of Kannada, Telugu and Malayalam language films. Khushboo was given his first opportunity in South Indian films by Kannada director Ravichandran.
Khushboo's devotees have built a temple for them in Tiruchirappalli. There is an idli in his name in Tamil Nadu called Khushboo idli. There is also a sari brand called Khushboo.
Khushboo is married to director and actor Sunder. After marriage, he converted to Hinduism. They have two children, Avandika and Anandita. They are permanent residents of Chennai.
Khushboo joined the DMK on May 14, 2010 in Chennai at a function attended by prominent leaders including Karunanidhi.
Some of what Khushboo said during the AIDS awareness campaign in 2005 was controversial. Khushboo has been controversial in saying that it is not wrong for girls to have safe sex before marriage and that no one has the right to insist that girls who get married be virgins. The statement was strongly opposed by two political parties and the matter was later taken to court.
