Elections to the 117-member Punjab Assembly were held on February 4, 2017. Punjab is currently ruled by the Shiromani Akali Dal-Bharatiya Janata Party alliance with Prakash Singh Badal as the Chief Minister. The turnout was 78.6%. Major parties contesting the 2017 elections: Indian National Congress, Aam Aadmi Party, Shiromani Akali Dal, Bharatiya Janata Party, Bahujan Samaj Party, Independents
