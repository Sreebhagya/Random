Rathinirvedam is a Malayalam novel written by Padmarajan. The book was published in book form in May 1970. The novel was made into films in 1978 and 2011.
In this novel, the author depicts the zeal of the youth who are eager to overcome the barriers of the society in the frenzy of pure love and physical attraction. The story takes place in an imaginary village called Chepattumukku near Kayamkulam. Pappu and Raduvechi are the central characters.
The book was first published in book form in May 1970. In 2005, D.C. The book was republished through the Books Library Series.
