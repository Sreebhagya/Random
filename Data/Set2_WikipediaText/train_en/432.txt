AM was a political leader who represented the Changanassery constituency in the first Kerala Legislative Assembly. Kallyanakrishnan Nair. CPI He entered the Kerala Legislative Assembly as a representative. He became active in politics in 1954 when he was elected as the Municipal Councilor of Changanassery as a law student.
He also served as the Councilor of Changanassery Municipal Corporation, President of Nagara Sevak Samithi, President of Kottayam District Agricultural Workers Council and teacher.
