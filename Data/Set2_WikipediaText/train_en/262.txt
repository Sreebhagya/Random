Kadanad Grama Panchayat is a gram panchayat, covering an area of ​​40.19 sq km and comprising of Kadanad, Ramapuram and Velilappally villages in Lalam block of Meenachil taluk, Kottayam district. The Gram Panchayat was officially formed in 1958.
