Mexico is a country in the North American continent (also known as the United States of America). The capital of Mexico is Mexico City. The neighboring countries are the United States, Belize and Guatemala. Mexico has the highest number of Spanish speakers in the world. Dahlia is the national flower of Mexico.
Different cultures evolved in pre-Columbian Mesoamerica before the beginning of European relations. Some examples are Olmec, Toltec, Aztec, and Mayan.
In 1521, Spain occupied and colonized the area. It was ruled by the Viceroy of New Spain. Later, in 1821, the colonies gained independence.
