Makaramanju is a 2010 biopic directed by Lenin Rajendran. Lenin Rajendran portrays some moments in the life of the painter Raja Ravi Varma. It is also the film of the epic character Pururuvar. The film stars cinematographer and director Santosh Sivan and Karthika Nair in the lead roles. The film won the 2010 Kerala State Government Award for Best Second Film. SB was credited for the costume design of the film as well as Yugapurushan. Satheesan also received the award.
Makaramanju is a critically acclaimed film. It is one of the five Malayalam films screened at the International Film Festival of India. The film won the FIPRESCI Award for Best Malayalam Film at the Kerala International Film Festival. The film was screened at several film festivals. The film was considered for the 2010 National Film Awards.
