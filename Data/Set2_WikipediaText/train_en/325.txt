I. is a Tamil romantic action thriller film directed by Shankar. Produced and distributed by Venu Ravichandran under the banner of Oscar Films. The film is scripted by Shankar himself and stars Vikram and Amy Jackson in the lead roles. Music is composed by AR Rahman. The film was produced at a cost of Rs 185 crore. The film also stars Suresh Gopi and Upan Patel in the lead roles.
