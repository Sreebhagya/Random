Saghlool Ragib Muhammad Najjar. Egyptian geologist, author and researcher of the Qur'an. He has authored more than 150 scientific studies and forty-five books in Arabic, English and French.
She was born on November 17, 1933, in Basseon, Egypt. He graduated from Cairo University in 1955 with high marks. He is fluent in Arabic, English, French and German and is also a prominent Egyptian geologist. He received his doctorate in geology in 1963 from the University of Wales in the United Kingdom. Najjar is the chairman of the Committee on the Study of the Scientific References in the Qur'an and the High Level Committee of the Islamic Department in Egypt. The main work is The Geological Concept of Mountains in the Qur'an.
