Chaya is the only Malayalam short film to be screened in the Marched category at the 2011 Cannes Film Festival. PV The film was produced by Anoop. K. Directed by Subhash. V.K. The film is shot by Subhash himself and is 29 minutes long. Vipin Babu, a sixth standard student, played the lead role of Unni in the film. Divya Das, Pauly Watson, Harish Perady, Kunjachan Njarakkal and Kailas Malipuram have played other roles. The film was shot at Kanthalloor in Idukki district. The content of the short film is the desires of a child wandering for a picture of his father who died prematurely from a snake bite.
