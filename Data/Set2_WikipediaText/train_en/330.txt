Abdul Hameed alias A. Hameed is an Urdu writer.
She was born in 1928 in Amritsar. And then went to Pakistan due to partition. His collection of short stories, Manzil Manzil, became famous during his time working for Pakistan Radio. He is the author of several short stories, novels and plays. He has also received other awards in Pakistan. Died April 29, 2011 in Lahore due to respiratory illness.
