Nagaravadhu is a 2001 Malayalam film directed by Kaladharan and starring Vani Vishwanath, Sai Kumar and Srividya in the lead roles. Under the banner of OK Productions, Mani c. Produced by Kappan, the film is distributed by OK Productions and Cherupushpam Release. G.C. The story of this film is written by Karaikal. Screenplay and dialogues by Rajan Kiriyath.
The songs written by Prabha Varma and Sudhamshu were composed by M. Jayachandran is. Background music is by Rajamani.
