Thakazhi Kuttan Pillai was a famous Kathakali musician in Kerala. Thakazhi Kuttan Pillai was the originator of the southern style of Kathakali music. Kuttan Pillai has performed with many prominent Kathakali artists in India and abroad.
Kuttan Pillai, who had been suffering from dementia for a long time, passed away on September 17, 2007 at 4 am.
