North Wayanad Assembly constituency consists of Mananthavady, Edavaka, Vellamunda, Thondarnad, Panamaram, Thirunelli and Thavinjal gram panchayats of Wayanad district and Kottiyoor and Kelakam gram panchayats of Kannur district. C. P. K in I. C. Kunhiraman has been representing the constituency since 2006.
The constituency was abolished in 2008 with the reorganization of the constituency.
