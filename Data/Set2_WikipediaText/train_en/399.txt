Amjad Ali Khan or Ustad Amjad Ali Khan was born on October 9, 1945 in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh. Hafiz Ali Khan, a musician at the Gwalior Palace, is the father and Rahat Jahan is the mother.
The Sarod musical instrument was designed by his family. Amjad Ali Khan has developed his own style in Sarod reading. His vocal music-based compositions and technical prowess made him one of the best Sarod readers.
He was awarded the Padma Shri in 1975, the Padma Bhushan in 1991 and the Padma Vibhushan in 2001. In 1989, he received the Sangeetha Nataka Academy Award. .
