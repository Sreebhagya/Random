
Yamaseratops is a dinosaur that lived during the Late Cretaceous. The fossil was found in the Gobi Desert in Mongolia.
The specimen of the Hollow type IGM 100/1315 is a partial skull. Some other fossil fragments found in 2002 and 2003 are also considered to be theirs. At the back of the head was a bone-covered frill.
They had a parrot-like lip. Like other serotopia dinosaurs, they are herbivores and are thought to have eaten cones and pine.
