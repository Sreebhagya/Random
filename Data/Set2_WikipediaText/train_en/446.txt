Shapoor Poyan is a founding artist from Iran. Works with art in New York and Tehran. He has performed extensively in the Gulf, Europe and the United States.
After graduating from Tehran with a bachelor's degree in painting, he studied in the United States. Participated in the Myconas Biennale in Greece in 2013 and the Kochi-Muziris Biennale 2014 in Greece in 2014. == Kochi-Muziris Biennale in 2014 The exhibition showcased the power of architecture from the region, the "Unthinkable Garden", and the "Peak Damon", reminiscent of the physiology of nuclear explosions.
