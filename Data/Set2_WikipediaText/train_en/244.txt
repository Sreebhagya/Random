Eepan Varghese was a Kerala Congress leader and former MLA. He was twice elected to the Kerala Legislative Assembly. He represented Palluruthy constituency in the 5th Kerala Assembly and Ranni constituency in the 8th Assembly. He was one of the active leaders in the growth of the Kerala Congress from its inception.
Eepan PC George, who was the Working Chairman of the Kerala Congress Joseph Group, shifted his activities to the Kerala Congress Secular when a new group was formed. Later, when the Kerala Congress Secular Group merged with the Mani Group, he was one of the four advisers. Eippen Varghese died on November 9, 2011 of liver cancer.
