Wandazhi Grama Panchayat is a gram panchayat, located in Alathur block, Alathur taluk, Palakkad district in the Indian state of Kerala. The panchayath is spread over the villages of Mangalandam, Wandazhi and Mudappallur and covers an area of ​​56.70 sq. Km. Wandazhi panchayat is bounded on the north by Alathur and Vadakancherry panchayats, on the south by Vellattiri hills, on the east by Nelliampathi panchayat, on the east by Ayilur and on the Melarkode panchayat, on the west by East Kancheri and Vadakancherry panchayats.
