Yahya bin Zakariya, also known as Yahya Nabi, is a prophet in Islam. Among Christians, he is known as John the Baptist. The Arab Christians call him John, the western Syriac Johanon, and the Eastern Syrian John. In Malayalam it is called John, in Latin it is called John, in English it is John and in German it is John.
According to the Qur'anic account, the scholar Zachariah, who was in charge of the care of Mary, the mother of the Prophet Jesus, was killed by the Roman governor Herod Antipas, who was a specially given son of God while his wife was barren.
John the Baptist was the last prophet in Christianity before Jesus. The Bible refers to John the Baptist as the prophet who came to prepare the way for Jesus. Jesus was baptized by John the Baptist.
