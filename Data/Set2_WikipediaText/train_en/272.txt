Ezhacheri Ramachandran is a prominent poet and journalist in Kerala. He has received many awards, including the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award.
He was born in Ezhacheri village of Ramapuram panchayath in Meenachil taluk of Kottayam district. He was the editor of Deshabhimani Weekly. She has won various awards for professional drama songwriting, including three state government awards. He has been a member of the Executive Committee of the Kerala Sahitya Akademi and a member of the Chalachithra Akademi. He is one of the State Vice Presidents of the Progressive Art and Literature Society. He is also the President of the Sahitya Pravarthaka Sahakaranasangham.
