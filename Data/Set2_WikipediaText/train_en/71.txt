Dhakoran Kalan is a village located in the Ajit Singh Nagar district of Punjab. 
According to the 2011 Indian Census, there are 180 houses in Dhakoran Kalan. The total population is 966. This includes 524 men and 442 women. The literacy rate in Dhakoran Kalan is 73.71%. This is below the state average of 75.84. The number of children under the age of 6 in Dhakoran Kalan is 118. This is 12.22 per cent of the total population of Dhakoran Kalan.
According to the 2011 census, 301 people are employed in various occupations. Of these, 284 were men and 17 were women. According to the 2011 census, 98.67 per cent of people consider their job as their main source of income but 58.47 per cent see their current job as a temporary source of income for the next 6 months.
