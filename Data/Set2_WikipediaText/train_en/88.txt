Rishinaradamangalam is a village in Palakkad district in the state of Kerala, India. The village falls under the Alathur Taluk. Rishinaradamangalam is one of the few temples in Kerala dedicated to Lord Narasimha. Rishinaradamangalam is one of the two villages where 'Kannampravela' is conducted.
