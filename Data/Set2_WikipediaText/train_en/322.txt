Shirwan National Park is a national park in Azerbaijan. The National Park was established on July 5, 2003, by the order of the President of Azerbaijan, Ilham Aliyev, in the area of ​​the Salyan Rayon Administrative District. The surface area of ​​this national park is 54,373.5 hectares. Shirwan National Park was established in 1969 on the site of the Shirwan State Reserve. The park aims to conserve and reproduce the Goethe Gazelle, waterfowl and the common flora of the Shirvin Valley.
