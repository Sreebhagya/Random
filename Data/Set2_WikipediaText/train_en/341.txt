The Silver Lotus is an award given to Indian cinema. The Golden Lotus and the Silver Lotus are the annual awards given to those who excel in Indian cinema. Its Malayalam translation is Velli Tamara. The National Film Awards were instituted in 1954. The awards are decided by the Directorate of Film Festivals under the direct control of the Government of India. The award is presented by the President.
The best film and director are in the Golden Lotus category. The following categories are included in the Silver Lotus.
Each of the best films will be selected from the languages ​​included in the Eighth Schedule of the Constitution. Similarly, the Silver Chakoram Award is given to a film in a language not included in the Eighth Schedule. The award has also been introduced in the non-film category.
