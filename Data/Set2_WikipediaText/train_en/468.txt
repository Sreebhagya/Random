
The Hindu is an English language daily newspaper in India. Started from Chennai in 1878. It started as a weekly newspaper. Became a daily newspaper from April 1, 1889. The Hindu, which started as a publication of the Madras Triplication Society, was bought by Kasturi Ranga Ayyankar in 1905. The Tamil newspaper The Hindu started publishing on September 16, 2013.
