Emphasis is on fishing by planting poles in a flowing lake and spreading nets between them.
This method of fishing is practiced in many parts of Kerala. 
This method is common in the backwaters of Kochi. This system has been used for fishing in the backwaters of Aroor, Arukutty Mattancherry, Vallarpadam and Panambukadu for many years. This method is also available in Kannur.
The advent of the National Waterway has affected the livelihoods of people who fish with nets. New LNG The move to restrict fishing in the vicinity of the terminal is also likely to go a long way in removing the nets. The Vallarpadam Container Project resulted in the removal of 327 nets.
