Mohamed Najib bin Tun Abdul Razzaq, also known as Najib Tun Razak, is the sixth and current Prime Minister of Malaysia. He has been the Deputy Prime Minister since January 7, 2007 and succeeded Abdullah Ahmad Badawi as the Prime Minister on April 3, 2009. He is also the President of the United Malays National Organization. He is the son of former Prime Minister Abdul Razak. In the 2013 elections, the Najeeb Razak-led party won 133 out of 222 seats. This is seven seats less than in the 2008 election. There are indications that he will not be able to continue in office for a longer period of time as his popularity has plummeted.
