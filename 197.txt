In Trithala Grama Panchayat, Palakkad District, Kerala, the Regulator cum Bridge is a regulator cum bridge constructed across the Bharathapuzha river connecting Trithala and Pallippuram.

Velliyankallu Heritage Park is located near the bridge.

The bridge was built at Velliyankal on the way to Koodalloor from Trithala. The bridge is 295 meters long and 9 meters wide. The regulator has 27 shutters and a platform with 27 motors to operate them. The project is aimed at providing drinking water to the region.
On September 2, 2007, Chief Minister V.S. Achuthanandan inaugurated the bridge. .
