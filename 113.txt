Vattapattu is a Mappila art form similar to Oppana. It is presented by men as women in Oppana. Vattapattu is a song sung by people to express their happiness in connection with a wedding.
It is a competitive event at the State School Youth Festival and other youth festivals.
The composition of the Vattapattu is from the time the bride is brought to the bride's house to the time of the compromise with the bridesmaids. It takes at least an hour to play Vattapattu in the traditional way
In the lower house, Kunhahammed, Kundumkaran Moithootty and Thattan Muhammed are famous Vattapattu artists.
