
The green-eyed wood frog is a species of frog in the genus Litoria. They belong to the family Hylidae and are scientifically known as Litoria genimaculeta.
They are mainly found in Australia, Indonesia and New Guyana. Habitats include temperate forests, temperate shrubs, sand dunes, intermittent rivers, intermittent freshwater swamps, grasslands, and farmland.
