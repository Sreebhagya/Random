Kaphurthala is a village located in the Kapurthala District of the state of Punjab. Khug, Kapurthala is located at a distance of 10 km from the district headquarters of Kapurthala. Khug is the Sarpanch who is the sovereign of Kapurthala village. The Sarpanch is the representative elected by the people.
According to the 2011 Indian Census, there are 117 households in Khug and Kapurthala. The total population is 612. This includes 318 men and 294 women. The literacy rate in Khug and Kapurthala is 60.78%. This is below the state average of 75.84. The number of children under the age of 6 in Khug and Kapurthala is 57. This is 9.31 per cent of the total population of Khug and Kapurthala.
According to the 2011 census, 177 people are engaged in various occupations. There are 169 males and 8 females. According to the 2011 census, 97.74 per cent people consider their job as their main source of income but 8.47 per cent see their current job as a temporary source of income for the next 6 months.
All the 290 persons in Khug and Kapurthala belong to the Scheduled Castes. 0 persons belong to Scheduled Tribes.
