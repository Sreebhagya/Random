Daniel Day-Lewis is a famous English film actor. He won the Academy Award for Best Actor for his performance in My Left Foot, There Will Be Blood and "Lincoln". BAFTA won the Screen Actors Guild Awards for his portrayal of Bill and The Butcher in Gangs of New York. Steven Spielberg's Lincoln won the 2012 Golden Globe Award. He became the first person to win the Academy Award for Best Actor three times for the same film.
